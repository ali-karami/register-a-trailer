You do not need to
create a trailer record

You only have to create a trailer record to register non-commercial trailers that weigh over 3,500kg in gross weight.

Under UK law you do not need to register your trailer, however you may be required to produce a UK Trailer Registration Certificate at foreign borders.

You can still create a trailer record if you want to.
