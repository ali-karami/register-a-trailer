Trailer registration declaration

You must register any trailers that:

  • travel abroad, weigh over 750kg and are used commercially
  • travel abroad, weigh over 3,500kg and are not used commercially

Trailers travelling to the Republic of Ireland, Spain, Cyprus and Malta are not required to register, unless they pass through a foreign country that has ratified the Vienna Convention.

It is optional to register for all other trailers.
